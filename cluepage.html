• Item 1: The Poster

    Working on the Clue marketing material was so much fun for me, because the characters and game are so iconic, but there was so much room for me to get creative with my ideas. I've featured the main poster here, but I also made specific posters for each of the main characters.

    Item 2: The Plum Poster

    The poster for Professor Plum was definitely my favorite of all the posters I made. Click on the poster to check out a PDF of all the character specific posters!

    Item 3: Sweatshirt

    For each show I worked on, shirts were made for each cast and crew member. I loved the Clue poster so much, and while there's a little more sheen on the back than I might like, I'm still very proud of this turned out.

    Item 4: The program

    Here I've highlighted here the program for Clue that I made. I was only asked to make a very basic program, but the aesthetics and subject matter of Clue was far too interesting for me to leave it at a basic program. So, the whole thing is set up like an evidence file, with secret codes and messages spread throughout. Click on the cover to check it out! This is one of my favorite things I've made.
