• The Rainbow Letters - Book Cover

    In 2021, I designed the cover for The Rainbow Letters: The Temerity To Believe. This was one of my first professional design experiences and I learned a lot about meeting client needs while maintaining my own artistic vision (although they didn't let me pick the font). I loved the aesthetics of this cover so much that I picked it as the theme for the miscellaneous page!


    This is the graphic I made for the 'Travel Simulator' folder poetry I made for my 2023 Journalism & Design Toolkit Class. The simulator is available for download here. The graphic was heavily inspired by retro postcards and I really love how it turned out.


    Viking Variety Tour - Posters

    My first project as Marketing Manager for the Inglemoor Drama department was a production of student written plays. After I designed the main poster, I decided I wanted to do speicalized posters for each of the student's plays. I've highlighted two of my favorite posters, one for Our Primary Problemand one for Promising Young Writer(The, in my opinion, hilarious Twilight spoof play that I wrote). Each individual poster also had a corresponding Instagram post.

    Main Poster

    This is the main poster. It incorporates elements of pretty much every show that was part of this production. I especially love the big logo (which is based on The Muppet Show logo). Of all the posters I made in my time as marketing manager, this was probably the one I agonized over the most. I had basically complete creative freedom, and it was my first poster so I knew it had to be good. Overall, I really loved how it turned out (this one still is hung up in my room).
